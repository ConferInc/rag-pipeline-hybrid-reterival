### Overall daily pattern (for ~2,000 kcal adult)

- **Core daily goals**
  - **Protein**: 1.2–1.6 g/kg body weight/day; spread across all meals (protein at every meal).
  - **Dairy**: ~3 servings/day, full‑fat, no/low added sugar (e.g., milk, yogurt, cheese).
  - **Vegetables**: ~3 servings/day, variety of colors (leafy greens, cruciferous, etc.).
  - **Fruits**: ~2 servings/day, whole fruits preferred over juice.
  - **Whole grains**: 2–4 servings/day; minimize refined grains.
  - **Healthy fats**: From whole foods and oils (e.g., eggs, nuts, seeds, olive oil, fatty fish).

- **Daily limits (soft constraints)**
  - **Added sugar**: Aim for 0; don’t exceed **~10 g added sugar per meal**.
  - **Sodium**: Generally **< 2,300 mg/day** for adults.
  - **Saturated fat**: **< 10% of total daily calories**.
  - **Ultra‑processed foods**: Avoid as much as possible (chips, packaged sweets, sugary drinks, etc.).
  - **Sugar‑sweetened beverages & energy drinks**: Avoid; prefer water and unsweetened drinks.

---

### Breakfast rules

- **Must include**
  - **Protein**: Eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, nut butter, tofu, beans, or leftover meat/fish.
  - **At least one of**:
    - **Dairy**: Milk, yogurt, cheese (full‑fat, no/low added sugar).
    - **Whole grains**: Oats, whole‑grain toast, whole‑grain cereal without added sugar.
- **Strongly recommended**
  - **Fruit**: 1 serving (e.g., whole fruit, berries on yogurt/oats).
  - **Healthy fats**: Nuts, seeds, avocado, olive oil, whole‑fat dairy.
- **Avoid/limit**
  - Refined‑carb only breakfasts (e.g., plain white bread, sugary cereal, pastries).
  - Sugary drinks (juice, sweetened coffee drinks); if juice, keep to small portion and dilute.
  - Added sugar **≤ 10 g** in the meal.

---

### Lunch rules

- **Must include**
  - **Protein**: Poultry, fish, meat, eggs, tofu, tempeh, beans/legumes.
  - **Vegetables**: At least 1–2 servings (salad, cooked veggies, mixed into mains).
- **Strongly recommended**
  - **Whole‑grain base or side**: Brown rice, quinoa, whole‑grain bread, whole‑wheat roti, etc.
  - **Dairy option**: Cheese, yogurt, or milk if not taken at breakfast.
  - **Healthy fats**: Olive oil dressing, nuts/seeds, avocado, fatty fish.
- **Avoid/limit**
  - Deep‑fried options; prefer grilled, baked, roasted, or stir‑fried.
  - Ultra‑processed sides (chips, packaged snacks).
  - Sugary beverages; stick to water/unsweetened drinks.
  - High‑sodium processed meats and sauces; keep within daily sodium budget.

---

### Dinner rules

- **Must include**
  - **Protein**: Similar sources as lunch; ensure daily protein target is met by the end of the day.
  - **Vegetables**: 1–2 servings (e.g., cooked vegetables, side salad, mixed veg dishes).
- **Strongly recommended**
  - **Whole grains or starchy vegetables**: Whole‑grain pasta, brown rice, quinoa, or root vegetables.
  - **Fruits**: Optional serving (e.g., a whole fruit as dessert or snack).
  - **Healthy fats**: Olive oil, nuts/seeds, fatty fish, full‑fat dairy.
- **Avoid/limit**
  - Heavy, ultra‑processed, or very salty convenience foods.
  - Sugary desserts and sweetened drinks; if dessert is included, keep added sugar **≤ 10 g**.
  - Excessive refined carbs without protein (e.g., large plain pasta portions, white rice only).

---

### Daily cross‑meal checks

- **Protein present at every meal** (breakfast, lunch, dinner).
- **Total vegetables ~3 servings/day** and **fruits ~2 servings/day**, spread across meals.
- **Dairy ~3 servings/day** across meals/snacks, unless contraindicated.
- **Whole grains 2–4 servings/day**, replacing refined grains when possible.
- **Cumulative added sugar** stays low (each meal ≤ 10 g, many meals at 0 g), sodium and saturated fat within daily limits, and **ultra‑processed foods are the exception, not the base** of the diet.
